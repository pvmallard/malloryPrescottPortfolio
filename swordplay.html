swordplay

Co-Creators: Em Albright, Nefeli Hadjiyiannis

Swordplay is a collaborative motion capture project utilizing the ATLAS Blackbox Theatre, visuals, audio, and programming.

My role in the group was the primary software developer and AI asset and sound manager. With assistance from the other members, I mainly worked on coding in Max, Stable-Diffusion, and Ableton, also on sampling audio files (found in the brainstorming link below).

swordplay was an interactive visual instalation in hosted in the ATLAS Blackbox Theater. The space was programmed to interact with the movements of two trackers attatched to swords so the users could experience a fight across different environments commonly seen in various media.

Link to Brainstorm Doc and Audio Files

The space was divided into four areas: fantasy, space, pirate, and video game. Each area had related background music and sound effects that paired with visuals created by AI through Stable Diffusion.

To orient the Blackbox Theater to the project, the visuals, lighting, and sounds were coded and implemneted on Max and Ableton to pair with the trackers.

The point of the project was to allow the user a playful experience of swordfighting in a fully immersive space.
