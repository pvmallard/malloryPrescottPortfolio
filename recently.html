recently in froggy minds

A visual design portrait project done using Adobe Lighthouse, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

recently in froggy minds is an independent visual portrait book inspired by the song "recently," by Liana Flores. The soft colors and visuals are influenced by the aesthetic and lyrics of the song.

The aesthetic was solidified by an impromptu photoshoot done all in one sitting, in the middle of the night, aided by a desk lamp and a purple folder. All the raw photos from the shoot were developed and edited in photoshop and illustrator. Many alterations were made, ranging from altering base settings like contrast and highlights, to overlaying the geometries from multiple layers to alter hue and style.

The intent of the visuals was to both reflect the lyrics, but apply a sense of nonsense. The term "froggy" was a play on "foggy", to reflect the status of a clouded mind. Often multiple photos were combined for additional affects.
