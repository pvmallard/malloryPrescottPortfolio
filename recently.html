recently in froggy minds

A visual design portrait project done using Adobe Lighthouse, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

I used it as a chance for me to play with colors and contrast on photos it took with nothing but a desk lamp and purple folder. I paired images alongside lyrics from Liana Flores’ “recently,” to create some dynamic photos. When given our prompts, I chose the one related to song lyrics, and so began the search for a song. I sorted through some I thought would fit best and started to plan out potential aesthetics and styles that could best fit each until I finally narrowed it down to “recently,” and the sort of soft pinks and purples as shown.

The aesthetic was solidified by an impromptu photoshoot done all in one sitting, in the middle of the night, aided by a desk lamp and a purple folder. That’s how I took all my photos, photoshop and illustrator helped me handle the rest, until I finally pieced it all together in lightroom. I used brushes for the letters and various lighting, but used a lot of the other settings for other subtle effects and the coloring, trying something different for each image I edited.

As someone who hates taking photos of myself, it was a fun way to try to get more comfortable doing so. The name ‘Recently in Froggy Minds’ came from both the song and a few other goofy things since these were some of the last photos I had taken of myself prior to a brain surgery that would help ease my ‘froggy’ mind. Plus I just like the stuffed animal frogs.
